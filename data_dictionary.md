Column Name,Data Type,Description
School_ID,Integer,Unique identifier assigned to each Chicago Public School.
Name_of_School,String,The official name of the school.
"Elementary, Middle, or High School",String,ES indicates elementary school; MS middle school; HS high school.
Safety_Score,Float,Student Perception/Safety score (1-99) from 5 Essentials survey.
Average_Student_Attendance,String,Average daily student attendance %.
ISAT_Exceeding_Reading_Percent,Float,Percentage of students who exceeded reading performance standards.
Community_Area_Name,String,The name of the Chicago community area where the school is located.
Community_Area_Number,Integer,The official index number for the specific neighborhood.
Latitude,Float,The geographic latitude coordinate of the school.
Longitude,Float,The geographic longitude coordinate of the school.
Ward,Integer,The City Council district number used to join school and crime datasets.
Crime_Count,Integer,Aggregated total of crimes reported in the corresponding Ward (2011-2012).
